## Description

GoBattleKit helps you prep for ranked PvP in Pokémon GO.

Point it at your Pokémon and find out which ones are worth building for ranked battles. Import a CSV from PokeGenie and the app checks every Pokémon against the IV spreads that deep-dive analysis has shown to matter in each league. No CSV on hand? Type a Pokémon in by hand and get the same answer.

GoBattleKit also trains the parts of PvP that come down to muscle memory. Quiz yourself on how many fast moves it takes to reach a charged move. Learn the timing that lets a slower move go off first. Drill type matchups until you stop having to think about them.

What you get:
- IV target checking for Great League, Ultra League, and Master League
- PokeGenie CSV import, or manual entry one Pokémon at a time
- Your own saved IV targets, with import from pogo-simulator deep dives
- Move-count, move-timing, and type-effectiveness quizzes

Your data stays on your device. The app downloads public game data and rankings from PvPoke and keeps your imported CSV and saved targets locally. There are no accounts and no ads. Nothing you do in the app is tracked.
