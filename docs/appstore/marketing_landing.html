GoBattleKit

A Pokémon GO PvP companion app

GoBattleKit helps you decide which Pokémon are worth building for ranked PvP, then helps you practice the parts of a battle that come down to timing and move counts.

What it does

  • Checks your Pokémon against the IV spreads that matter in Great League, Ultra League, and Master League
  • Imports a PokeGenie CSV, or lets you enter a Pokémon by hand
  • Stores your own IV targets, with import from pogo-simulator deep dives
  • Quizzes you on move counts, move timing, and type matchups

Your data stays with you

The app downloads public game data and rankings from PvPoke. Your imported CSV and saved targets stay on your device. There are no accounts and no ads, and nothing you do is tracked.
