# GoBattleKit

A Pokémon GO PvP companion app for iOS and Android, built with [BeeWare](https://beeware.org/).

## Features

- **Move Count Quizzes** — practice counting fast moves to charge attacks in Great, Ultra, and Master League
- **Optimal Move Timing Quiz** — learn when to throw charge moves to minimize free turns given to your opponent
- **Type Effectiveness Quiz** — practice type matchups
- **PvP IV Checker** — import your PokeGenie CSV and check your Pokémon against curated PvP IV targets
- **My PvP IV Targets** — set your own custom IV targets and check your Pokémon against them
