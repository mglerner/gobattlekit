GoBattleKit

A Pokémon GO PvP companion app

GoBattleKit is free because the Pokémon GO PvP community is built on generosity. It stands on PvPoke's data and on the IV research that so many people share. If you find the app useful and want to say thanks, a small tip is always appreciated!

Tip via Venmo @mglerner

You can also support the project by sharing it with your PvP friends, or leaving a review on the App Store.

Built by Michael G. Lerner (TitanTrainers15)
Game data and rankings from PvPoke by EmpoleonDynamite (MIT license)
IV research by Ryan Swag, JRE47, DragapultSim, and HomeSliceHenry, via GamePress and Pokémon GO Hub
Battle sims from pogo-simulator
